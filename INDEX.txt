💡 使用技巧

• 将 QUICK_REFERENCE.txt 添加到 shell 别名:
  echo "alias ocref='cat /root/openclaw-tool/QUICK_REFERENCE.txt'" >> ~/.bashrc

• 创建快捷命令:
  echo "alias ocdeploy='bash /root/openclaw-tool/openclaw-deploy.sh'" >> ~/.bashrc
  echo "alias ocfix='bash /root/openclaw-tool/openclaw-troubleshoot.sh'" >> ~/.bashrc

• 定期运行健康检查:
  bash /root/openclaw-tool/openclaw-troubleshoot.sh --auto
